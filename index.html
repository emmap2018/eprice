About Me


I currently work at Island Records, part of Universal Music Group, in London as the Commercial & Streaming Manager.

Among other responsibilities, it's my job to lead day to day relationships with key physical and digital business partners, including Apple Music and iTunes, Amazon, Deezer and more. Whilst at Island I've worked on campaigns for global acts such as Drake and Ariana Grande, for UK acts such as Mumford & Sons and Ben Howard, and for up-and-coming artists such as Sigrid, Dermot Kennedy and Easy Life. 

Whilst I've enjoyed my time working for Universal Music, as I approach my 5 year anniversary working with the company, I know it's not what I want to do forever. I enjoy the creative and problem-solving sides of coding and, having spoken to friends who have re-trained as coders and having given a lot of thought to re-training myself, I'm extremely excited about coding and the change of lifestyle it could give me.
